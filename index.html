Our Story

Where Tradition
Meets Artistry

Masala Chowk was born from a simple but powerful vision — to create a place where families gather, flavors shine, and every visit feels like coming home. Founded by Rajesh Radio in the heart of Bundi.

Our kitchen is a canvas where North Indian comfort meets South Indian delicacy, Chinese vibrancy, and Continental elegance. Every dish tells a story.
